Architecture

To get image embeddings, I trained 5 CNNs using the fast.ai `cnn_learner`, utilizing transfer learning from ResNet50 (available from the fast.ai library). The architecture and weights from ResNet50 are cut at the last convolutional layer, where several additional layers are added. These additional layers are trained using my training dataset to perform a specific classification task. After training the last layers with the ResNet50 weights frozen for several epochs, I unfroze the pre-trained weights and fine-tuned them to my data using smaller learning rates (so weight adjustments were small).
